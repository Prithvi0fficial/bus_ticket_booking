Ticket Cancellation Policy

1. Cancellation Window

  • Tickets can be cancelled up to 2 hours before the scheduled departure time.
  • No cancellations are allowed within 2 hours of departure.

2. Refund Policy

  • Full refunds will be issued for tickets cancelled more than 24 hours before departure.
  • 50% refund for cancellations made between 2 and 24 hours before departure.
  • No refund for cancellations made less than 2 hours before departure or after the bus has departed.

3. How to Cancel

  • Login to your NGarage account and visit the 'My Bookings' section.
  • Click on the ticket you'd like to cancel and select the "Cancel Ticket" option.
  • Refunds for eligible cancellations will be processed to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.

4. Special Conditions

  • Tickets booked with promotional discounts or coupons may not be eligible for full refunds.
  • NGarage reserves the right to modify the cancellation policy at any time without prior notice.
